# Tarot Card Primer

## Tarot Deck Structure

A standard tarot deck has **78 cards**:
- **22 Major Arcana** (The Fool to The World)
- **56 Minor Arcana** (4 suits of 14 cards each)

---

## Minor Arcana (Used in Game)

### Four Suits (Tribes)

#### Pentacles (Earth)
- **Theme**: Wealth, material success, practicality
- **Game Mechanic**: Gold generation, economy buffs

#### Cups (Water)
- **Theme**: Emotions, relationships, healing
- **Game Mechanic**: Healing, support effects

#### Swords (Air)
- **Theme**: Conflict, intellect, action
- **Game Mechanic**: Direct damage, aggression

#### Wands (Fire)
- **Theme**: Passion, energy, creativity
- **Game Mechanic**: Buffs, action effects

### Card Ranks
- **Ace**: Beginnings (Tier 1 in game)
- **2-10**: Numbered progression
- **Page/Knight/Queen/King**: Court cards (higher tiers)

---

## Major Arcana (Future Heroes)

Examples:
- **0. The Fool**: New beginnings, risk
- **I. The Magician**: Skill, power, manifestation
- **II. The High Priestess**: Intuition, secrets
- **III. The Empress**: Abundance, nurturing
- **IV. The Emperor**: Authority, structure

---

## Symbolism

- **Elements**: Earth, Water, Air, Fire
- **Numbers**: Numerology significance
- **Colors**: Emotional/thematic associations
