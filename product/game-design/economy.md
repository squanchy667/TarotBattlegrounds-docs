# Economy System

## Gold Sources

| Source | Amount | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Per Turn | +1 (capped at 10) | Starting 3 gold |
| Selling Cards | +1 | Per card sold |
| Pentacles Synergy | +1 | When selling Pentacles |
| Readings (optional) | +3 (rare) | Random bonus |

---

## Gold Sinks

| Sink | Cost | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Buy Card | 3 gold | Standard purchase |
| Reroll Shop | 1 gold | Refresh cards |
| Tier Upgrade | 5 gold (decreasing) | Unlock next tier |
| Readings (optional) | 2 gold | Random bonus |

---

## Balance Philosophy

- **Early Game**: Tight economy (3-4 gold/turn)
- **Mid Game**: More flexible (6-8 gold/turn)
- **Late Game**: Abundant (10 gold/turn, capped)
