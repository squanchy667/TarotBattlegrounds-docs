# Core Gameplay

## Game Concept

4-player auto-battler with tarot theme

## Gameplay Loop

1. **Recruit Phase** (45-60s)
   - Buy cards (3 gold)
   - Sell cards (1 gold)
   - Reroll shop (1 gold)
   - Position board (max 7 cards)

2. **Combat Phase** (Auto)
   - Board vs board simulation
   - Damage to losing player
   - Check eliminations

3. **Repeat** until one player remains

## Win Condition

Last hero standing (eliminate all opponents)

## Core Mechanics

- Gold increases per turn (cap 10)
- Tavern tiers unlock over time
- Tribes: Pentacles, Cups, Swords, Wands
- Card tiers: 1-6 (higher = stronger)
