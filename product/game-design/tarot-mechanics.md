# Tarot Mechanics

## Tarot Theme Integration

### Suits as Tribes

#### Pentacles (Earth)
- **Theme**: Wealth, material resources
- **Synergy**: +1 gold on sell
- **Color**: Green/Gold

#### Cups (Water)
- **Theme**: Healing, emotions
- **Synergy**: Heal adjacent minions for 2
- **Color**: Blue

#### Swords (Air)
- **Theme**: Aggression, conflict
- **Synergy**: TBD (piercing damage?)
- **Color**: Silver

#### Wands (Fire)
- **Theme**: Action, energy
- **Synergy**: TBD (buff attack?)
- **Color**: Red/Orange

---

## Major Arcana as Heroes

(Phase 4 feature)

- The Fool: "Random card generation"
- The Magician: "Double next card's effect"
- The High Priestess: "Discover a secret"
- The Empress: "Buff all minions +1/+1"
- The Emperor: "Gain 2 gold"

---

## Minor Arcana as Cards

- **Aces**: Tier 1 basic cards
- **2-10**: Tiered progression (2-3 = Tier 2, 4-5 = Tier 3, etc.)
- **Pages/Knights/Queens/Kings**: Tier 4-6 powerful cards

---

## Readings (Optional Bonus)

Pay 2 gold for a random bonus:
- +3 gold
- Discover a card
- Upgrade a random card
- Refresh shop for free next turn
